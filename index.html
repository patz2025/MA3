My UCF Journey

My journey at UCF has honestly been alot better than what I expected it to be.

Before making my move to Florida, I was living in South Korea to take some time to travel, while studying for my Associates Degree online.

Living in a different country and only being able to communicate with family and friends online made me want to start making videos of my daily life in Korea. I would have a lot of fun editing videos, taking photos and adding my own personality to it.

After seeing what I was able to do with the programs that were available online, I started playing around with different programs and found a love for finding a way to bringing my visions to life.

As time went by and I was making more personal projects, I decided to start looking into what kinds of job opportunities there were for these type of skills. I found out that my dream company to work for were hiring for people skilled for social media work. that i grew up with had many open positions for positions in the creative department.
